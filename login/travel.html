COONOOR, a quaint hill station in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, is famous for its tea plantations, scenic viewpoints, and cool climate.

RAMESWARAM, located on Pamban Island in Tamil Nadu, is a sacred pilgrimage site known for its temples, beaches, and the iconic Pamban Bridge.
